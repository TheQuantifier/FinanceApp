============================================================
HOW TO LIST MODELS ANYTIME (WINDOWS POWERSHELL)
============================================================

1. Open PowerShell
2. Run:

curl `
  -X GET `
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" `
  "https://generativelanguage.googleapis.com/v1beta/models?key=$env:GEMINI_API_KEY" `
  | Select-String '"name"'


OR simpler:

Invoke-WebRequest `
  "https://generativelanguage.googleapis.com/v1beta/models?key=$env:GEMINI_API_KEY" -UseBasicParsing


============================================================
HOW TO LIST MODELS ON MAC / LINUX
============================================================

curl -s \
  "https://generativelanguage.googleapis.com/v1beta/models?key=$GEMINI_API_KEY" \
  | grep '"name"'

HOW TO SWITCH MODELS
============================================================

To change which Gemini model your Finance App uses:

------------------------------------------------------------
1Ô∏è‚É£ Update your local .env file
------------------------------------------------------------
Open `.env` and change the model:

GEMINI_MODEL=models/gemini-2.5-flash
(or ANY option from the list above)


------------------------------------------------------------
2Ô∏è‚É£ Update Render environment variables
------------------------------------------------------------

On Render:

1. Go to your **Finance App ‚Üí Environment** tab  
2. Find `GEMINI_MODEL`  
3. Change value to the desired model, e.g.:

   models/gemini-2.5-flash  
   models/gemma-3-4b-it  
   models/gemini-2.0-flash  

4. Click **Save**  
5. Click **Deploy Latest Commit** (needed for env changes)
